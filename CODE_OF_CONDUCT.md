# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone who participates in the JSONMorph project. This Code of Conduct sets expectations for behaviour.

## Expectations
- Be respectful of differing opinions, experiences, and backgrounds.
- Offer constructive feedback and be open to receiving it.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project.
- Use inclusive language.

## Unacceptable Behaviour
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.
- Demeaning or derogatory comments, personal attacks, or trolling.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Any illegal or unethical conduct.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please contact the maintainers at `security@example.com`. Include relevant details so we can investigate.

## Enforcement
Maintainers will review reports promptly and take appropriate action. This may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community.

## Scope
This policy applies to all project spaces, including GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other project-related communication.

Thank you for helping make JSONMorph a welcoming community for everyone.
